Yoga

For soul and body

Yoga is one of the most popular methods of exercise and meditation in recent years. It combines physical postures, controlled breathing and meditation, as well as healthy approaches. It is a practice that can be practiced by anyone, men and women alike, and originated in India. It is a physical, spiritual and mental practice that has been practiced by millions of people in many different places around the world for centuries.
